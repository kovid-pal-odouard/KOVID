Revolutionary Cultures and their Literatures: A Comparative Analysis

On revolution, scholarship, and methodology

Much of the scholarly literature on revolutions seeks to answer the question: why there and at that time, rather than most other places at most other times?

Oppression, inequality, poverty, and arbitrary rule have been aspects of human societies near-ubiquitously for centuries, yet the vast majority of the time those conditions alone are not enough to lead people to revolution.
